# Token Revenue Treasury Product Layer

Date: 2026-07-08

## 1. Why ALPHA Exists

ALPHA is the protocol coordination asset for Alpha Protocol. Its product role is to connect governance participation, staking participation, protocol incentives, contributor coordination, Green Label ecosystem alignment, and risk reporting / dispute governance participation.

ALPHA must not be presented as a guaranteed-profit asset, a fixed-yield product, a dividend promise, an insurance product, or a token price appreciation promise.

## 2. Protocol Revenue Flow

The intended product loop is:

1. Protocol revenue is generated by Green Label fees, protocol services, future creator fees, and ecosystem revenue.
2. Revenue enters Treasury V2 for transparent on-chain accounting.
3. Treasury V2 applies the protocol split.
4. DAO / Security Layer / timelock / execution queue oversee sensitive policy and execution paths.

Current Phase 2B-1 scope is read-only product presentation. It does not add Mainnet revenue collection, token purchase flows, or any on-chain write action.

## 3. Treasury V2 Split

Treasury V2 uses the following USDC split:

- 50% Relief Pool.
- 20% Buyback / Burn Pool.
- 20% Builders / Contributors Pool.
- 10% Staking Rewards Pool.

Treasury V2 has been verified on Devnet for USDC four-pool distribution.

## 4. Staking Rewards Are Not Guaranteed Yield

Staking V1 has verified stake / claim / unstake flows on Devnet.

The staking rewards pool receives 10% of the protocol revenue split, but staking rewards are protocol-rule-based incentives. They depend on pool balance, protocol parameters, and future governance.

The product must not show guaranteed APY, fixed yield, guaranteed returns, or dividend-style promises.

## 5. Relief Pool Is Not Insurance

The Relief Pool is intended for victim protection, risk response, Green Label slash proceeds, and future DAO-reviewed relief policy.

The Relief Pool does not mean automatic insurance, fixed compensation, or a guaranteed payout. Any future payout policy must be defined by protocol rules and governance.

Green Label is not insurance, not a credit rating, and not investment advice.

## 6. Builders Pool Governance

The Builders / Contributors Pool is intended for:

- Contributors.
- Protocol maintenance.
- Audits.
- Ecosystem operations.
- Future DAO-approved spending.

Builders spending should be governed and auditable. It should not be controlled arbitrarily by a single wallet for Mainnet production.

## 7. DAO Oversight

DAO / Security Layer oversight should cover:

- Treasury parameters and split policy.
- Builders spending.
- Relief policy.
- Staking reward policy.
- Green Label refund / slash decisions.
- Emergency controls and timelock-protected execution.

Sensitive actions should route through governance, timelock, queue, and payload checks before Mainnet production use.
